Icon Generator

Generates all 8 PNG icon sizes required by the PPPD Tracker PWA. Click the button below — your browser will download each file. Move them into the icons/ folder.

After downloading:

  1. Create an icons/ folder inside pppd-tracker/
  2. Move all downloaded icon-*.png files into it
  3. Serve the pppd-tracker/ folder with any local HTTP server
    (e.g. npx serve . or python3 -m http.server 8080)
  4. Open the URL in Safari on your iPhone
  5. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
